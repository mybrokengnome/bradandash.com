Wedding Details

When & Where

Our ceremony will be held on the beach of The Grand Plaza on St Pete Beach at 5:30PM, with a reception to follow. The Grand Plaza rises high over St. Pete Beach offering sweeping 360 degree views of the beach, Gulf of Mexico and bay.

We have set up an Exclusive Group Attendee Code to make it easy for you and your guests to book hotel room reservations at your special discounted rates. To make reservations online please go to: www.grandplazaflorida.com, click on Reservations and use Group Attendee Code WE2757.

No kids please.

The Venue

Ceremony will begin at 5:30PM on the beach front of The Grand Plaza. A reception will follow afterwards in one of the ballrooms.
What to Wear

The only rule is no shoes at the ceremony.
It's a beach wedding in Florida. Be prepared for warm weather, sun, a night of partying & dancing.
Parking

There is a parking lot on premises of The Grand Plaza, as well as public lots close by.
